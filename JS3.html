JavaScript Project 3
Webcam
This project is a basic photo booth app using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It displays a live video feed from the user's webcam and lets them take photos by clicking a "Take Photo" button. The captured photos appear below the video feed, and a "snap" sound plays each time a photo is taken. Users can also download their pictures after taking them.
